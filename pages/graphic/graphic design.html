Graphic Design

51 Studio

A personal favourite project of mine was this project for UTC Plymouth while I was an intern at 51 Studio, an advertising agency based in Plymouth.
Reimagining logo variations and creating a poster for the client within the constraints of the brand's identity regarding font, style and theme was a great creative exercise, giving me my first experience with Adobe Illustrator and sparking a love for the busy atmosphere of a creative agency.

University of Plymouth

The assignment here was to redesign handouts which the university could use on their open days for their design-based course. This was one of my first independently led live client projects and was a great basis for understanding briefs, pitching, receiving client feedback and delivering content as promised.

The Dumb B*tch Bible

A small design that was used as artwork to accompany an original podcast idea in my first year of university. The irreverant title reflects the tone of the podcast: an off the cuff, agony aunt style show that seeked to make the listener laugh just as much as answer their questions.

The Slayin' Game

Some of my first work with Adobe Photoshop during A-Levels. My assingment was to create a poster from an original film concept. I was able to incorperate some flair into this horror-comedy idea while exercising technical ability in both photography and design skills.
