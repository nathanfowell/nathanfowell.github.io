YA YA Music Video Treatment

This director's treatment was made in response to a second year brief where we could create a pitch deck of our choosing.
I chose Beyoncé's 'YA YA' as a peice of work that I saw a vision for after hearing the song, infusing the music video idea with references that embodied the history and cultures that Beyoncé was channeling in the track and wider album, 'Cowboy Carter.'

The Maker Challenge 2022

For my first university project, I was tasked to show how I see myself as a creative.
I decided to create this stop motion GIF to challenge myself in the integration of various skills and techniques and think this demonstrates my flare for post-production well.

VR Battle Chess

You can scroll down through this treatment to visualise my virtual reality Battle Chess idea that was made in my first year of university.
The treatment uses AI created imagery to help the reader visualise my idea and is a great example of how I wish to present my projects in the future.
